Comprehensive Disease Prediction

Our AI models assess risk factors for multiple critical health conditions.

Heart Disease

Assess your risk for coronary artery disease, heart attacks, and other cardiovascular conditions based on:

  • Blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Family history and lifestyle factors
  • ECG results and other diagnostic data

Kidney Disease

Evaluate your kidney function and risk for chronic kidney disease using:

  • Blood urea and creatinine levels
  • Urine analysis results
  • Medical history and comorbidities

Diabetes

Predict your risk for Type 2 diabetes mellitus based on:

How AI Health Predict Works

Our simple process makes comprehensive health assessment accessible to everyone.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up as a patient, doctor, or administrator based on your needs. Our system will guide you through the appropriate onboarding process.

2

Complete Your Health Profile

Provide your health information. Patients enter their metrics, doctors set up their practice information, and administrators configure system settings.

3

Get Your AI Assessment
